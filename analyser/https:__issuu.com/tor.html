What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of information placed on your computer or mobile device that uniquely identify your browser or device.

Why do we use cookies?

At Issuu we use 4 different kinds of cookies: essential, functionality, performance and advertising cookies.

Essential cookies help make our sites usable by enabling basic functions like page navigation, login and access to secure areas of the website. Issuu cannot function without these cookies.

Functionality cookies enable us to remember information that changes the way the website behaves or looks, like your preferred language or the region you are in. We also use these cookies for testing new features and how Issuu looks.

Performance cookies help us to understand how visitors interact with our websites by collecting and reporting information anonymously. These cookies also help us provide accurate reading statistics for our publishers.

Advertising cookies are used to track visitors across websites. The intention is to display ads that are relevant and engaging for the individual user and thereby more valuable for publishers and third party advertisers.

Opting out of cookies

If you wish to opt out of cookies, please follow these guidelines.

To opt out of essential, functionality and performance cookies, please disable cookies for Issuu.com in your browser settings.

To opt out of advertising cookies, go to http://optout.networkadvertising.org .
